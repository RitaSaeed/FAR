Happy Tails

These are the stories that remind us why we do what we do. From rescue to forever home, each happy tail celebrates a life changed through adoption.

Lucky

Lucky came to us at just four weeks old after being abandoned from a moving vehicle. We treated him, got him healthy, and now he’s thriving and playing happily in his forever home!

Smudge

Smudge lived under a porch in the middle of winter, rarely coming out where anyone could see her. After two months of trying, we were finally able to bring her to safety. She has since been adopted and now has a new kitty sibling and best friend!

Sunshine

Sunshine was found alone on the railroad tracks, frightened but full of heart, and she immediately stole ours. Now she’s living her best life with her three best friends by her side.
