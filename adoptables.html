Adoptable Animals

Paws, whiskers, and wagging tails await!
These adoptable animals are ready to steal your heart and your couch.

Rosie

Rosie showed up at our home after being on her own for a while. Now healthy and spayed, she loves being indoors and soaking up affection — and she’s ready for her forever home.

Winken

Winken was once adopted but sadly ended up back on the streets. Thankfully, his original rescuer found him again, and now he’s looking for a true second chance at a forever home. He’s such a sweet boy and deserves a family who won’t give up on him.

Shade & Shadow

Shade and Shadow have been with us since birth and are about 3 months old. This best-friend duo is incredibly cuddly and loves being together. 

Elbee

Elbee was found at a young age and was very afraid of people at first. With time and patience in her foster home, she’s slowly adjusting to indoor life. She just needs a loving, patient home where she can feel safe and let her true personality shine. 

Adoption Options

Our adoption options are designed to create confident, lasting matches between cats and their future families.

Meet & Greet

Schedule a meet-and-greet so you can spend meaningful time with the cat you’re interested in. This helps ensure the personality, energy level, and connection feel like the right match for both of you.

Foster-to-Adopt

If you’d like extra reassurance, our foster-to-adopt option gives you and your cat time to adjust before making the adoption official, helping ensure a lasting match. We will provide food and litter during this period



Adopt!

When you know you’ve found the right fit, fill out our adoption form and email it to us at <email> to start the process. We thoughtfully review every application to ensure each cat is placed in a safe, committed home
