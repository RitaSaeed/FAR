Our Mission


At Forgotten Animal Rescue, we exist for the ones no one sees. We are a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization founded out of heartbreak — and hope. In our community, stray and feral cats have been surviving without resources, protection, or anyone fighting for them. We saw suffering that had become “normal.” We saw kittens born outside with no shelter, injuries left untreated, and fear where there should have been safety — and we decided that normal wasn’t acceptable.

We believe no life deserves to suffer simply because they were unwanted, abandoned, or born outside. Every cat — whether friendly, frightened, stray, or feral — deserves dignity, medical care, nourishment, and compassion. Our mission is to rescue stray and abandoned cats from unsafe environments, provide medical care, spay and neuter services, and rehabilitation, reduce overpopulation through responsible intervention, educate our community about humane solutions, find loving and committed homes for adoptable cats, and protect and advocate for feral colonies through TNR (Trap-Neuter-Return).

We are not just rescuing animals — we are breaking cycles. We are creating change where there were once no options. Forgotten no more.

Our Adoption Policy

This policy is used to ensure that all of our animals are going to safe and loving homes. When adopting a pet with us, we ask that you sign off on these agreements below

Medical Records

All of our cats and kittens are:
 - Spayed or neutered
 - Tested negative for Feline Leukemia (FeLV) and Feline Immunodeficiency Virus (FIV)
 - Up to date on age-appropriate vaccines and preventatives

Contract Agreements

 - The cat will be kept indoors only
 - The cat will never be declawed
 - If the cat ever needs to be rehomed, they must be returned to us or we must approve the new home


Adoption Fee

 Our adoption fee is $115, which helps us recover a portion of the veterinary and care expenses for each kitty.
